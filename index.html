Vision

Montessori Academy's vision is for each student to develop:

  • A Love of Learning
  • Creativity
  • Character
  • Independence and Self-Discipline
  • Respect for Self, Others, and the Community
Mission

To provide a quality Montessori education in a safe, nurturing environment.

Passion

Our passion is our students. Our degreed and certified Montessori teachers have a passion for teaching children ages 3 through 8th grade.
